What I found during my quest, and what I report here, is meant to be purely anecdotical and it is not necessarily following a logical and sound educational path. The hope, with which I build this blog, is that it will eventually become a useful reference for me to look back at and that also other people, with a similar background to mine, may find some use out of these notes and notebooks. My name is Lorenzo Posti and I'm currently a postdoctoral researcher at the [Strasbourg Observatory](https://astro.unistra.fr/) (France) specialized in models of galaxy dynamics, which I use to study the origin of galaxies and the nature of dark matter.
